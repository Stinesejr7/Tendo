WHAT IS TENDO?

Tendo is a soft robotic exoskeleton, imagine a robotic glove, which strengthens your grip. It covers the thumb, index finger and forearm and it’s equipped with soft artificial tendons and smart sensors detecting when you are about to grasp an object and applies the requested strength to your fingers. Tendo creates greater independence for people suffering from limitations due to e.g. arthritis, an injury, stroke or age. Tendo results in an improved self-image and quality of life as well as a reduced need of assistance from the community and family. Tendo is worn almost like a glove and enables a strong grip in more or less all everyday situations.

HSOFT EXOSKELETON

It is a soft robotic system with intelligent sensors that detects and enchances your grip with the help of artificial muscles

ONE-TWO FINGERS

Minimizing size, weight and cost by reducing number on involed fingers - result of user oriented developement and tests.

FOR PEOPLE

.. not symptoms. Tendo is created with the user in mind and with an understanding of how a disability affects the self-image.

OUR MISSION

OUR STORY

The purpose of Tendo is to create freedom and to open up new opportunities for those who can’t live the life that others take for granted, where actions such as holding a fork at a restaurant or opening up a bottle of water becomes a struggle. By creating artificial strength, for the hands and grip, in the shape of a discreet product that challenges the traditional medical devices of today’s market, Tendo offers more freedom and an enhanced self-reliance.
The product is under development and more information will follow later on.
